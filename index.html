What is a Smart Home?

A smart home uses electronic devices connected through the internet to automate lighting, security, temperature control and more.

Popular Smart Devices

Smart Lighting

Control brightness and color using mobile apps or voice commands.

Smart Security

Includes cameras, sensors and alarms for real-time monitoring.

Smart Thermostat

Automatically adjusts temperature to save energy.

Smart Speakers

Voice-controlled assistants for daily tasks.

Smart Plugs

Turn traditional devices into smart ones.

Smart Door Locks

Keyless entry with mobile authentication.
